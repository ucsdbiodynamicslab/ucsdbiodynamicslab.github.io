SPIRAL CORE OSCILLATIONS IN RAYLEIGH-BENARD CONVECTION

(simulation of the Swift-Hohenberg equation coupled with mean flow)

Snapshots of the order parameter and corresponding vorticity

Notice a large positive vorticity blob in the center and vortex pairs at dislocations. vortex pairs pull the dislocations (and the whole spiral body due to wavevector frustration) in clockwise direction, whereas the central blob rotates the core in the opposite direction.
