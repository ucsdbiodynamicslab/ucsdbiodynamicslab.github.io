Spiral-Defect Chaos (SDC) in Rayleigh-Benard convection:

Experimental facts

What you need:

  • Small Prandtl number ( ~1)
  • Large aspect ratio ( >20)
  • Moderate supercriticality ( 0.5..0.7)

What you get:

Morris, Bodenschatz, Cannel and Ahlers, 1993 , CO2 Assenheimer, Steinberg, 1993, SF4 near critical point
